Kobe Bryant

What makes a hero? A hero is someone that inspires you to be someone greater than yourself. Someone that you turn to when times get tough.

The hero that I most admire has the following characteristics:

  • Hard working - they work hardest when no one is watching
  • Makes the people around him better - knows what makes the people around him tick
  • Dedicated - does everthing with excellence
  • Humble - they do not tell their own stories
  • dependable - takes credit in bad and good times

To me, no man exemplifies these values more than Kobe Bryant.
